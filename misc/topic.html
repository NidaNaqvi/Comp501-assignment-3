Chosen Technology/Topic

Importance:

Doing something to stop the spread of fake news is important because it can have significant effects on our society. When people believe false or misleading information, it can affect their decisions and have a tremendous impact on society. Fake news can also lose people's trust in our trusted news outlets, making it more difficult for them to differentiate between reliable and unreliable sources of news. Fake news is critically important as it can be intentionally used to sway public opinion on critical matters, such as elections, public policy, or social issues. This can harm the democratic process because people are swayed by false and misleading information, and they are unable to make informed judgments based on factual and reliable information. Overall, it is crucial to prevent the spread of fake news, as it is a fundamental building block for our society to make informed judgments based on factual and reliable information.

We specifically chose the topic of fake news because of its relevance in today's media landscape, especially with the widespread consumption of news through social media.
